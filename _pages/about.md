I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the [IDRA Research Group](https://idra.unitn.it/) at Università di Trento (UniTN), supervised by Prof. Daniele Fontanelli. My current research focuses on optimization for robotics and human robot collaboration, combining learning and model-based methods to enable more capable and reliable autonomy. I obtained my Ph.D. in Mechanical and Automation Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2024, advised by Prof. Ben M. Chen, and my B.Eng. in Automation from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2020.
